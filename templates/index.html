How BurnFit AI Works

This web dashboard uses a high-performance XGBoost Regressor machine learning model to predict active caloric burn based on individual biometric and physical attributes.

📊 Key Mathematical Drivers:

  • Workout Duration (45% Weight): The absolute longest driver of thermodynamic energy conversion.
  • Heart Rate (35% Weight): Direct biological indicator of oxygen uptake and cellular respiration.
  • Body Temperature (12% Weight): Direct physical measure of heat generated by muscular friction and metabolic sweat triggers.
  • Biometric Factors (8% Weight): Weight, Age, Height, and Gender establish the basal active metabolic rate.

🛡️ Scientific Validation:

The model was trained on a dataset of 15,000 active individuals with direct supervision using a test partition to verify its accuracy. On average, the predictive error is just 1.48 calories (MAE), giving this website near-lab precision!
